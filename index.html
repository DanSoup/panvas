v Functions

drawPixel(x, y);

Draw a single pixel at a point. The point will be within the boundary of the pixel.

drawLine(firstPoint, secondPoint);

Draw a straight line from one point to another.

drawBezierCurve(start, end, controlPoints);

Draw a bezier curve with a number of control points.
